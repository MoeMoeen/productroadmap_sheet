Revenue
= qlub GMV × Monetization Rate


qlub GMV
= # Restaurants × GMV per Restaurant × Net Adoption Rate


GMV per Restaurant
= Total Transactions per Restaurant × Average Order Value


Total Transactions per Restaurant
= f(Traffic to Restaurant, Fulfillment Rate)


Traffic to Restaurant
= Discovery Traffic × Discovery Conversion Rate


Discovery Traffic
= Organic Discovery Traffic + Triggered Discovery Traffic


Organic Discovery Traffic
= Organic New Customers + Organic Returning Customers


Triggered Discovery Traffic
= Incentive-driven Discovery Traffic
+ Social-driven Discovery Traffic
+ Word-of-Mouth-driven Discovery Traffic
+ Marketing-driven Discovery Traffic
+ Loyalty-driven Discovery Traffic


Returning Customers
= Repeat Rate × Existing Customer Base


Net Adoption Rate
= f(Gross Adoption Rate, Checkout Completion Rate)


Monetization Rate
= f(Relative Qlub Bargaining Power, New Monetisation Models)
